About

At Hyde Law Office, we advocate for families in South Florida: Broward County, Fort Lauderdale, Miami-Dade County, Palm Beach and other South Florida communities who need competent legal help with family, domestic relations and financial problems. Our mission is to provide professional, experienced and compassionate guidance to help you resolve your family law related problems.

Our Practice Areas

We have three main areas of practice: Family Law: This includes marital agreements, paternity issues, divorce and separation, child support, custody, asset and property division, and support or visitation modifications. Estate Planning/Probate: Estate planning includes everything from wills and trusts to guardianships, powers of attorney and health care surrogates and issues, and dealing with probate court. Guardianship: Guardianship of Adult or Minor child is a legal process of appointing a guardian, a competent person to be responsible for the care, custody and control of a vulnerable or incapacitated person and/or their property.

Our Focus

At Hyde Law Office, we focus on exercising professional and independent judgement. The services you receive from us will be completely candid and private. We focus not just on legal issues, but issues of ethics, morals, and social and political factors that are relevant to you and your situation. We help you to achieve your goals of moving your life, your family forward in a positive direction. We truly care about our clients and are here to help you every step of the way through the legal process, and provide the best possible results. We’re here to empower you to seek resolution to your difficult problems, and focus you on finding a better future during your time of adversity.
